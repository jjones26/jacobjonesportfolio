01. About

Who I Am

I'm a developer actively looking for my next opportunity — whether that's freelance work, a full-time software dev role, or a game dev position. I bring a mix of front-end development, game scripting, and a genuine love for building things that work well and feel good to use.

I build games in Godot and Unity, write C# and GDScript, and have been expanding into full-stack development through freeCodeCamp. As a former teacher, I bring strong communication, problem-solving, and a learner's mindset to everything I build.

Off the screen I'm a proud Dad, a Buffalo Bills fan (Go Bills!), an avid golfer, and a fantasy football enthusiast. If you have an opportunity — big or small — I'd love to hear about it.
