01. About

Who I Am

I'm a developer driven by the challenge of bringing complex ideas to life through code. Whether I'm scripting game logic in Godot or Unity using GDScript and C#, or diving into full-stack development — I've always been a lifetime learner.

As a former teacher, I enjoy educating others and building positive learning communities. When I'm not at my desk, I'm wearing my most important title: Dad. I'm also a huge Buffalo Bills fan (Go Bills!), an avid golfer, and a fantasy football enthusiast.

If I'm not coding or gaming, I'm probably fixing my swing on the course or managing my roster for the next big win.

Shipped The Daily Crostic — a daily word puzzle that refreshes every 24 hours. The hardest part wasn't the puzzle logic, it was making sure the daily seed stayed consistent across time zones. Learned a lot about date handling in GDScript. Give it a play and let me know what you think.
